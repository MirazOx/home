July Movement coverage

In 2024, I covered Bangladesh's July Movement and its aftermath — the student-led uprising that led to the fall of Sheikh Hasina. This collection gathers the field reports, misinformation checks, post-uprising politics, accountability stories, and Netra investigations/interactives connected to that moment.

Reporting through those weeks came at a cost: on 4 August I was attacked while filing for TBS — named in a Committee to Protect Journalists alert the next day and the subject of a longer TBS Panorama feature.
