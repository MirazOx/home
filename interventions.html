Workshop facilitator · Digital Citizenship and Misinformation Resilience Program · ULAB × British Council

Cure with Facts

In March and April 2025, I ran a month-long misinformation resilience campaign that culminated in a workshop session on platform misinformation and source verification for undergraduate students. The intervention was designed to help participants recognise how false or misleading content circulates online, and how stronger verification habits can make them more resilient readers and sharers.
